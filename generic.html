Excel as a
Data Analyst

The software's basic statistical functions and charting capabilities enable quick exploratory data analysis and visualization of trends. Pivot tables empower analysts to aggregate and summarize data, providing meaningful insights. Excel is also widely used for creating reports and dashboards, facilitating collaboration by allowing analysts to share their findings with team members and stakeholders.
